Frequently Asked

How do you verify helmet condition?

Each helmet undergoes a 15-point inspection covering the outer shell, inner liner, retention system, visor mechanism, ventilation, and safety certifications. Only helmets that pass every checkpoint receive our certification.

Can I sell my helmet through Elite?

Yes! We accept consignment submissions from verified sellers. Fill out the contact form above selecting "Sell My Helmet" and our team will reach out within 48 hours.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day no-questions-asked return policy on all purchases. Items must be returned in the same condition they were received.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Orders over $300 qualify for complimentary express shipping. Duties and taxes may apply depending on your country.
